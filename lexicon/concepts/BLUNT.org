** DEFINITION

INTENSELY THICK so as to be UNABLE TO CUT.

** NOTES

*** Old Chinese Criteria
[GENERAL/SPECIFIC]

1. The current general word for bluntness of any kind is dùn 鈍.

[GENERAL]

2. Cuò 挫 refers primarily to the act of making an object blunt.
