** DEFINITION

UPSIDE of a HEAD.

** NOTES

*** Old Chinese Criteria
1. Diān 顛 is the old current word for the crown of the head.

2. Dǐng 頂 is a later word for the crown of the hed which slowly became current from Warring States times onwards.
