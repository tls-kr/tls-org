SEEM to DRINK by CAUSING to ENTER ONE'S MOUTH by BREATHING IN.

** NOTES

*** Old Chinese Criteria
1. The current word for sucking something clean is shǔn 吮.

2. Shuò 唶 is a rare dialect word for sucking at something.
