** DEFINITION

RUBBISH which SINKS to the BOTTOM of LIQUIDS RESEMBLING WINE ETC..

** NOTES

*** Old Chinese Criteria
1. Zāo 糟 refers to unfiltered wine full of dregs.

2. Pò 粕 refers to the wine dregs only.

*** Huang Jingui
HUANG JINGUI 2006

FOOD 43.

糟，未濾之酒。

粕，濾酒後所剩之滓粕。
