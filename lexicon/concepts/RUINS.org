** DEFINITION

BUILDING OR BUILDINGS that have been COMPLETELY DESTROYED.

** NOTES

*** Old Chinese Criteria
1. The current standard word for ruins is xū 墟 / 虛.

2. Qiū 丘 "mound" is sometimes used periphrastically to refer to ruins.
