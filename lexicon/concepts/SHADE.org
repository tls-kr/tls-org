** DEFINITION

PLACE LACKING LIGHT of SUN SURROUNDED BY PLACES WITH LIGHT OF SUN.

** NOTES

*** Old Chinese Criteria
1. he general area of shade is yīn 陰 (ant. guāng 光 "light")

2. Yīn 蔭 refers specifically to the shadow of a tree.
