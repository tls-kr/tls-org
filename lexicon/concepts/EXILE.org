** DEFINITION

LEAVE the STATE or CAUSE TO LEAVE the STATE.

** NOTES

*** Old Chinese Criteria
1. The standard word for voluntary exile is wáng 亡.

2. Chū 出 is a polite periphrastic term occasionally used for going into exile.
