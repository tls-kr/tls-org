** DEFINITION

SMALL SHINING BALL which GROWS IN MUSSELS.

** NOTES

*** Old Chinese Criteria
1. The standard word for a rounded pearl is zhū 珠.

2. Jī 璣 refers to a smaller variety of a non-round pearl.
