** DEFINITION

TERRITORY CONTAINING MANY TREES AND SHRUBS EVERYWHERE.

** NOTES

*** Old Chinese Criteria
1. The standard word for a copse or forest of any kind is lín 林.

2. Cóng 叢 refers to a thicket consisting of shrubs and trees.
