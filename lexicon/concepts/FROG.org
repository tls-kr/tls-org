** DEFINITION

GREEN OR BROWN AQUATIC ANIMAL which has LONG LEGS AND CAN JUMP. [RANA]

** NOTES

*** Old Chinese Criteria
1. General term for a frog is wā 蛙.

2. Měng 黽 is a specific term for one kind of a frog.
