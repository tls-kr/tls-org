** DEFINITION

LACK GUILT.

** NOTES

*** Old Chinese Criteria
1. The standard idiomatic phrase for innocence is bù gū 不辜.

2. Wú zuì 無罪 is a very common periphrastic phrase for general innocence.

*** Modern Chinese Criteria
無辜
