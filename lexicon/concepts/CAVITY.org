** DEFINITION

A DEEP HOLE IN a SURFACE.

** NOTES

*** Old Chinese Criteria
1. The general term for a hole with a cavity beneath it, natural or artificial, is xuè 穴.

2. Kǒng 孔 refers to a small cavity or aperture.
