** DEFINITION

BIG BROWN WILD BEAST, DANGEROUS TO HUMANS. [BIOLOGY]

** NOTES

*** Old Chinese Criteria
1. The current general term for a bear is xióng 熊.

2. Pí 羆 tends to refer to the brown bear.

3. Xióng pí 熊羆 refers to a bear of any sort.
