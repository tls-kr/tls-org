** DEFINITION

DEVELOP OR CHANGE so as to BECOME MORE EXCELLENT.

[[FORTSCHRITT]]

** NOTES

*** Old Chinese Criteria
1. The current general word denoting progress of any kind is yì 益.

2. Jìn 進 is very occasionally used to denote progress.
