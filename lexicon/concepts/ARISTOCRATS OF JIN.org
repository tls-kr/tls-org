HF: Zhì Bó 智伯 ( 知伯 ), Earl of Zhì (died 453), notoriously ambitious and all-powerful Prime Minister Jìn 晉 during the early reign of Jìn Āi gōng 晉哀公 Duke Āi of Jìn (reigned 456-438).
