** DEFINITION

LOVE for one's SELF:own STATE.

** NOTES

*** Old Chinese Criteria
The modern keyword 愛國 "patriotic" has an interesting history in China from ZGC-times onwards. 愛國心 "patriotism" comes in a poem by 汪懋麟 quoted in HYDCD.
