** DEFINITION

TOUCH USING one's TONGUE CAUSE TO BECOME MOIST.

** NOTES

*** Old Chinese Criteria
1. The current word for licking is shì 舐.

2. Shǔn 吮 adds to the notion of licking that of sucking.

NB: Tiǎn 舔 is post-Buddhist.
