** DEFINITION

MYTHICAL ANIMAL OR SNAKE which has FOUR LEGS AND RIDES ON CLOUDS.

SACRIFICE 22.

龍是種綜合多種動物特徵的圖騰崇拜物。

螭，無角龍。
