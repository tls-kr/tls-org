** DEFINITION

A TOOL PRODUCED USING METAL, USED FOR PLASTERING WALLS.

** NOTES

*** Old Chinese Criteria
1. Wū 圬／釫 and màn 鏝／槾 are different dialect words for the trowel, which could be made of metal or of wood.
