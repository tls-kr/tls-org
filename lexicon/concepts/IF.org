* =CONCEPT= IF
:PROPERTIES:
:CUSTOM_ID: uuid-2d3d0b50-ba72-473d-9cda-a9db0cc81a52
:TR_ZH: 如果
:END:
** DEFINITION

RELATION between sentences S1 and S2 such that it is NOT TRUE that S1 AND NOT S2.
