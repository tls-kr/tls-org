** DEFINITION

ADULT who is NOT MARRIED.

** NOTES

*** Old Chinese Criteria
1. There are no competing terms for "bachelor" in classical Chinese. The current word is kuàng fū 曠夫 (opp. yuàn nu# 怨女 "unmarried woman of marrigeable age").
