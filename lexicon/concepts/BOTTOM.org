** DEFINITION

PART of an ARTEFACT or THING that is MOST LOW.

** NOTES

*** Old Chinese Criteria
1. The current word for the bottom of a vessel is dǎng 當.

2. Dǐ 底 is a Han dynasty dialect word for the bottom of a vessel.
